Story

Our Love Story

First Meet

November 1st, 2019

In 2019, amidst the bustling corridors of ITS, we crossed paths as new colleagues. Each of us was already committed to another. Little did we know that four years hence, we would walk the path of singledom, only to find our hearts intertwined in the most unexpected of romances.

Opening A New Chapter

End of 2022

In 2022 we were brought together as a team in the ITSProvement Competition. Through shared struggles, we discovered harmony with each other and embarked on our journey of love together in 2023.

Weathering The Storm

2023

Our relationship endured through challenging times. Yet, with every obstacle we faced, our bond continued to flourish, granting us the sacred ground to blossom personally and romantically.

Engagement

February 17th, 2024

In February 2024, we solidified the seriousness of our relationship with an engagement ceremony, marking a significant step in our journey of love filled with commitment and the growing happiness shared between us.
